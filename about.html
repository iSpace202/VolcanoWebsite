Origins

2012

Just like every other zoomer kid, we were obsessed with Minecraft. We [Volcano's founders] met through Minecraft and created some of the worst maps ever, yet we loved every second of it. We stuck to it, got better at it every year, and in 2016 we released The Colossus, a map which Mojang accepted to the Realms catalogue.

Beginning

2018

In 2017, Microsoft started the Marketplace Program. The following year, we began working with Pathway Studios to publish our creations on the Marketplace. The following year, we were accepted to the Marketplace Program, becoming the first team from the MENA region to partner with Microsoft on the Marketplace Program, and the rest was history.
We created a plethora of Minecraft Maps, ranging from adventures, minigames, and survival spawns, some of which have been featured on Minecraft's official social media.

Eruption

2024

Today, we are a fully fledged game development studio.
